Tonkatsu

Description

Japanese Dish that consists of breaded, deep-fried pork cutlet. It involves coating pork slices with panko and frying them in oil. Often served with shredded cabbage and rice

Ingredients

  • 2 Boneless Pork Loin
  • 3 Cups Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Flour
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 0.5 Cup Panko

Directions

  1. Put a whisked egg, flour, and panko in different bowls
  2. Dip the pork in flour, egg, then panko
  3. Heat the oil to 350 Degrees F
  4. Gently put the piece of pork into oil and cook for 1 minute each side until golden brown
