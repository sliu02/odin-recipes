Shrimp Fried Rice

A shrimp fried this rice

Ingredients

  • 1lb Shrimp uncooked
  • 1 Tsp cornstarch
  • 3 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 0.25 Cups Green Onion finely chopped
  • 5 Cups Leftover White Rice
  • 12 Oz Frozen Carrots and Peas
  • 2 Tbsp Soysauce
  • 1.5 Tsp Sesame Oil

Directions

  1. Add shrimp, cornstarch, salt, and pepper to marinate 10 minutes
  2. Cook shrimp for 1 minute each side until cooked
  3. Beat eggs into different skillet on medium heat
  4. Stir in rice
  5. Place in soysauce and sesame oil
  6. Add shrimp and frozen peas/carrots
  7. Cool for 10 minutes and serve
